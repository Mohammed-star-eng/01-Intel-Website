Carbon Neutrality

Intel is committed to achieving net-zero greenhouse gas emissions in its global operations by 2040.

Water Restoration

Restoring more water than it consumes in manufacturing operations by funding watershed projects.

Renewable Energy

Over 90% of Intel’s global electricity use comes from renewable sources as of recent reporting.

Green Building

Intel facilities meet LEED certification standards with smart, energy-efficient design elements.

Supply Chain Sustainability

Partnering with suppliers to reduce Scope 3 emissions and implement circular economy strategies.

Eco-Innovative Packaging

Reducing plastic, improving recyclability, and designing smarter packaging for electronics.

Product Efficiency

Intel designs chips that deliver more performance per watt to lower the energy footprint of computing.

Waste Reduction

95% of Intel’s manufacturing waste is recycled or reused instead of being sent to landfill.

Climate Advocacy

Intel collaborates with governments and NGOs to shape bold, science-aligned climate policies.
